Story

Follow Aloy, our fearless heroine, as she ventures into the majestic yet treacherous terrains of the Forbidden West. With the world teetering on the brink of collapse, she faces a dual challenge: to uncover the mysteries of her past and to combat a looming threat that could spell the end of everything. Allies await in the most unexpected places but so do formidable new enemies. Every step into the unknown requires wisdom, bravery, and a will to survive against all odds.

World

Horizon Forbidden West invites you to explore a lush, post-apocalyptic world where nature has reclaimed the ruins of a forgotten civilization. From the sprawling deserts to the dense jungles and towering mountains, each landscape is alive with mysterious machines and hidden dangers. Interact with diverse cultures, ride machines across the land, soar through the skies, or dive into the depths of the oceans. Discover a world filled with beauty, mystery, and adventure at every turn.

Combat

In Horizon Forbidden West, your survival depends on mastering a versatile combat system. Choose your path as a hunter using precise bow and arrow attacks from a distance, embrace the shadows with stealth to take down unsuspecting foes, or stand your ground with a melee build that lets you face enemies head-on. Utilize Aloy's knowledge to exploit elemental weaknesses, turning the tide of battle in your favor. Every encounter demands strategy, quick thinking, and adaptability.
